SCP-20

Picture of SCP-20.

Item #: SCP-20

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: It is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell. The lighting in the cell must be dimmed to prevent its capture. It should be fed once every two weeks by agents wearing protective armor. Any portals that open in this cell are to be filled once again.

Description: SCP-20 is a male humanoid with a pale blue, leech-like body. Its total body mass is 1kg, but its mass is distributed unevenly. It has two spherical eyes and six tentacles.

It cannot be seen by the naked eye and must be seen using a special lens. Despite this limitation, it can be located with ease. The exact method of perception is currently unknown, although it has been observed to appear in various locations at great speeds.

The only characteristic that can be observed of SCP-20's mind, aside from its method of perception, is that it spends most of its time wearing a grin on its face. The type and number of teeth possessed by SCP-20 is unknown. It beings to regenerate every day at 10:00 AM Pacific Time.

SCP-20 feeds off of pieces of fabric and plastic which emerge from portals within its cell. It requires between 18 and 60 man hours to produce each piece of material.

Testing was conducted to determine what affect the increased mass of SCP-20 would have on its abilities. However, instead of showing any change in behavior, SCP-20's power was doubled in force three days after it was released from Containment Cell V1 (see Experiment Log 20-A). Despite this change, it did not increase in volume or mass.
