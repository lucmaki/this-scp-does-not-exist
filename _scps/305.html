SCP-305

Picture of SCP-305.

Item #: SCP-305

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-305 is currently uncontained due to the lack of any viable method of containment. The Foundation has taken the unusual step of denying reports of SCP-305's existence by media outlets in order to prevent confusion with other incidents.

Description: The exact nature of SCP-305 is currently unknown.

The location at which SCP-305 manifests is an independently owned Waffle House restaurant located in the state of Georgia, United States. When a subject attempts to enter the location, they will witness barstools being overturned by an unidentified form of force and large quantities of food being thrown out of the establishment. At this point, no cell phones appear inside the establishment to record any footage for later broadcast.

Upon exiting the establishment, witnesses will report a crowd of bystanders waiting outside the building. The witnesses will invariably deny that there was a disturbance at all until asked about it by security personnel.
