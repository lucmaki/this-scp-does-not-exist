SCP-50

Picture of SCP-50.

Item #: SCP-50

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: The Foundation is to monitor any and all sightings of SCP-50. Further information will be processed by the technical division.

Description: SCP-50 is a fleet of ███ unidentified vehicles, which all display characteristics of an off-road military grade vehicle. Any attempts to gain information about the drivers or designation of origin have failed.

SCP-50 appears as a fleet of five vehicles. A large object resembling a bus or monorail is located at the front of the fleet, containing all crew members. The vehicles of SCP-50 vary between models and styles, with different models in the fleet having different model numbers and names. All units are human-operated and were originally used by a private company, USA Transport Corporation.

SCP-50 is considered non-anomalous as it has been observed in various parts of the world, including Russia and Mongolia. The Foundation is currently investigating where such vehicles originated from.

['Addendum:

The USA Transport Corporation was an American company founded in 20██ by █████ ████████. USA Transport Corporation had a long history of business in the transportation sector. It had access to high-tech vehicles, including fleets of semi-autonomous vehicles and drivers rarely seen before.

On 11/04/20██, USA Transport Corporation was closed down due to financial reasons, and all vehicles were sold to a new company, M████ Incorporated. M████ Incorporated was owned by █████ ████████, who was a (Deceased).

Due to the fact that all vehicles have been sold, all records on the fleet are currently classified.']
